About

Josh McFadden

Josh is a New York based photographer and filmmaker, and a student at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts and Leonard N. Stern School of Business, double majoring in film and marketing. Growing up in Nebraska instilled a passion for exploring far and wide to find untold stories, and his move to the big city serves as a constant source of inspiration to capture big moments. His work is focused on visual storytelling as a tool for communication, branding, and problem-solving.

That work spans fashion photography along with commercial, corporate, and narrative projects. This includes campaigns and lookbooks for labels like Yutori, Kozaburo, and ViewFinder on the photo side, and spec ads, documentaries, and short films on the video side. Across all of it, the goal is the same: translating complex ideas into clear, story-driven, engaging content for the modern media landscape.

Have a project, a campaign, a short film, or a story that needs telling? Josh would love to hear about it.
