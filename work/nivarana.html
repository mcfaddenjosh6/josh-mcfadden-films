Nivarana

Nivarana was a final project Joshua wrote and directed for a Fall 2025 class at NYU Tisch Film School. Nivarana tells a story about drug abuse through the long journey home of two roommates. They are carrying a mirror across NYC after hours, and by the end of the film, the mirror reveals more than just reflections. Enjoy!
