Jazz

Written, directed, and edited by Joshua, JAZZ was a project for a documentary strategies class that he took in the Spring of 2026. It visualizes a lost love story between a man and a woman, and his old saxophone. It exemplifies Joshua's writing ability as well as visual storytelling to convey strong emotion through filmmaking.
